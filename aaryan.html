Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr. (born 20 May 1983), popularly known as NTR Jr., is an Indian actor, producer, and television presenter who primarily works in Telugu films.[1] He has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2012.[2][3] Referred to in the media as the "Man of Masses", he has starred in 30 films.[4] Tarak's accolades include three Filmfare Awards South and two Nandi Awards.
